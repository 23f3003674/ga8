Financial formulas

Weighted Average Cost of Capital:

\[ \text{WACC} = \frac{E}{V} \cdot R_e \;+\; \frac{D}{V} \cdot R_d \cdot (1 - T) \]

Dividend Discount Model (Gordon Growth):

\[ P_0 = \frac{D_1}{r - g} \]

Net Present Value:

\[ \text{NPV} = \sum_{t=1}^{n} \frac{CF_t}{(1+r)^t} \;-\; C_0 \]

Briefly define variables: E/D/V are equity/debt/firm value; R_e/R_d costs; T tax rate. Tie formulas to your assumptions slide.

Analyst notebook snippet


import pandas as pd

df = pd.read_csv("quarterly_metrics.csv")

# Compute YoY growth
df["rev_yoy"] = (df["revenue"] / df["revenue_prev_year"] - 1) * 100

# EPS trend signal
eps_ma_4 = df["eps"].rolling(4).mean()
signal = (df["eps"].iloc[-1] > eps_ma_4.iloc[-1])

print(f"Latest YoY revenue growth: {df['rev_yoy'].iloc[-1]:.2f}%")
print("EPS above 4-quarter MA:", signal)
